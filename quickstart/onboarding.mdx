# Onboarding Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you and your team get started with our platform quickly and efficiently.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

To begin, you'll need to create an account:

1. Visit our [sign-up page](https://example.com/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Verify your email through the confirmation link
4. Complete your profile setup

<Callout type="tip">
Use your work email address to ensure you get access to all team features automatically.
</Callout>

### Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

Once your account is created, you'll want to set up your workspace:

- **Choose a workspace name** that represents your team or project
- **Invite team members** by sending them email invitations
- **Configure your settings** to match your team's preferences

### Step 3: Connect Your First Integration

Our platform works best when connected to your existing tools:

<TabGroup>
  <Tab title="GitHub">
    Connect your GitHub repositories to automatically sync your documentation.

    1. Go to Settings → Integrations
    2. Click "Connect GitHub"
    3. Authorize the connection
    4. Select repositories to sync
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Slack">
    Get notifications and updates directly in Slack.

    1. Go to Settings → Integrations
    2. Click "Connect Slack"
    3. Choose your workspace
    4. Configure notification preferences
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="API">
    Connect via our REST API for programmatic access.

    ```bash
    curl -X POST https://api.example.com/integrations \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d '{"type": "github", "config": {...}}'
    ```
  </Tab>
</TabGroup>

## Common Tasks

### Inviting Team Members

To invite new team members:

1. Navigate to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer)
5. Click **"Send Invitation"**

They'll receive an email with instructions to join your workspace.

### Creating Your First Page

1. Click **"New Page"** in the sidebar
2. Choose a template or start from scratch
3. Add your content using our markdown editor
4. Add metadata (title, description, tags)
5. Click **"Publish"** when ready

### Setting Up Custom Domain

1. Go to **Settings → Domains**
2. Click **"Add Custom Domain"**
3. Enter your domain name
4. Follow the DNS configuration instructions
5. Verify your domain ownership

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Access Workspace

If you're having trouble accessing your workspace:

- Check that you're using the correct email address
- Verify your account is activated
- Contact your workspace administrator
- Check your email for invitation links

### Integration Not Working

If an integration isn't working:

- Verify your API credentials are correct
- Check the integration status in Settings
- Review the integration logs for errors
- Ensure you have the necessary permissions

### Content Not Publishing

If your content isn't publishing:

- Check that you have Editor or Admin permissions
- Verify the page isn't in draft mode
- Review any validation errors
- Check your publishing workflow settings
